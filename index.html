Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017. The group consists of eight talented members who are known for their self-produced music, powerful performances, and unique sound that blends hip-hop, EDM, and rock elements.

Since their debut in 2018, Stray Kids has gained international recognition for their authenticity and artistic freedom. They are heavily involved in the writing, composing, and producing of their music, with the production team 3RACHA (Bang Chan, Changbin, and Han) leading the creative process.

Known for their intense choreography and meaningful lyrics that often address themes of youth, identity, and self-discovery, Stray Kids has built a dedicated global fanbase called STAY. Their discography showcases their versatility and growth as artists, making them one of the most exciting K-pop groups of their generation.
